Reflection


As a future system administrator, learning how to manage enterprise servers is significant because it can assist the system administrator in executing tasks easily and seamlessly. Additionally, understanding Ansible is crucial for server management because it simplifies task execution and automation implementation. In this course, the Prelim Period was the easiest for me since it only involved setup and basic scripting using Ansible. When it comes to the Midterm Period, I faced challenges in completing some activities because there were missing dependencies needed to accomplish those tasks. However, I understand how it works; I just felt that I lacked time to practice. Lastly, I enjoyed the Final Period because it mainly focused on installations. I also gained knowledge about how OpenStack functions and its importance in cloud services. Overall, I enjoy this course and appreciate our kind and understanding professor. I will forever appreciate your guidance during my learning journey. I appreciate your efforts in ensuring that your students excel and creating a pleasant learning environment.
